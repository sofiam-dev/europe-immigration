Why Choose This Path

Why Europe?

Europe offers a stable economy, excellent quality of life, world-class healthcare, and access to the EU single market. As a U.S. citizen, you can leverage your skills and capital to build a new life in one of the world's most desirable regions.

Why Through Business?

European countries offer long-term residency permits to entrepreneurs. By starting a company, you gain a direct and reliable path to legalization in Europe – with multi-year permits and a clear route to permanent residency.
